Top 3 Places to Visit in The United States

1. The Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty, formally known as “Liberty Enlightening the World”, was a gift from France. This gift was sent to celebrate 100 years of Franco-American friendship. The statue was sculpted by Frederic Auguste Bartholdi while the framework was designed by a French engineer, Gustave Eiffel, who also designed the Eiffel Tower.



2. Hollywood sign

The Hollywood Sign is a landmark and American cultural icon located on hills overlooking Los Angeles, California. First put up in 1923 as an advertisement for the nearby real estate development "HollywoodLand," the sign, minus the last syllable, became a cherished symbol for the Hollywood film industry.



3. Grand Canyon

It is the result of constant erosion by the Colorado River over millions of years, Most of the Grand Canyon lies within Grand Canyon National Park and is managed by the National Park Service, the Hualapai Tribal Nation and the Havasupai Tribe.
