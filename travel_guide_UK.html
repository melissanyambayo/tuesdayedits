Top 3 Places to Visit in the UK

1. The Tower of London

The Tower of London is a 900 year old castle and fortress in Central London - it holds the Crown Jewels which are said to be worth between three and five billion pounds. The Tower is guarded by twelves Beefeaters

2. Stonehenge

Stonehenge is a prehistoric monument located in Wiltshire, UK. Archaeologists believe that it was constructed from 3000 BC to 2000 BC. Which makes the monument older than the Pyramids in Egypt. It consists of around 100 upright stones placed in a large circle. Nearly 1 million people visit Stonehenge every single year.


3. The Angel of the North

The Angel of the North is located in the North of the UK, just outside Newcastle. It is a steel structure of an angel, 20 metres tall with wings that measure 54 metres across. It is Britain's largest scultpture and thought to the biggest sculpture of an angel in the world.
